Ga-Mashie Youngster's Civic Education Club

You are warmly welcome

This club is under the National Commission for Civic Eduaction. It is located at James town, Accra. The founder and coordinator for this noble club is in the person of Mr. Gabriel Ofori. The club came into existence in the year 2010 and it's motto was "Together we shall succeed". The club was established to help the youth to be responsible leaders in the community for the year's ahead.The club has been able to generate responsible peoples in the community, to mention a few, Ishmeal (Teaching Assistant in KNUST), Mr.Neurtey (Assistant Headmaster in Paladium School, Accra), Kwartei (In Military), Mr. Jonathana Quarcoo (Business man), Joyce Ofori (Level 400, University of Winneba), Samuel Ofori (Level 400, University of Ghana). The slogan for the club is "Ga-Mashie! Ambassador's of the law!!!".

Our Missions

Education. Education is the key to success. Promoting education through reading, learning and applying it in our modern world.

Constitution. Knowing the constitution helps us as individuals to know our rights and freedoms. Having the constitutional knowledge prevents people from infriging on your right.

Teenager pregnancy. A teenager pregnancy is the most pregnancies that has been occuring in our community. We are battling against this so that this teens can focus on their education and their life to become a better person in life.

Sanitation. Our community is not neat and clean so we do this to make sure that we have a conduicive environment. As the saying goes, Cleanliness is next to Godliness. We need to keep our environment clean to desist any diseases.
